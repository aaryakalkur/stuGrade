About Us

Welcome to stuGrade

At stuGrade, we are dedicated to providing students with an intuitive and efficient tool for managing their academic grades. Our platform helps students keep track of their grades, calculate averages, and organize their academic performance effectively.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students by giving them the tools they need to understand and improve their academic performance. We believe that with the right tools and information, students can achieve their educational goals and excel in their studies.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every student has easy access to reliable and user-friendly tools for managing their education. By continuously innovating and improving our platform, we strive to make a positive impact on students' academic journeys.

Our Team

Our team is composed of passionate individuals who are dedicated to enhancing the student experience. We come from diverse backgrounds and bring a wealth of experience in education, technology, and design to create the best possible product for our users.
